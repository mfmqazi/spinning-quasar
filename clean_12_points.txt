[OCR Extracted Content] 12 Point Guidelines

1. No animal food, No eggs, No Dairy.
2. No refined or highly processed foods. No Oil, No Sugar.
3. Eat a variety of foods from all five food groups. Herbs & spices are important.
4. Eat daily a minimum of 1% of your body weight in fruits (must include berries) and 1% in vegetables (must include green leafy vegetables).
5. Watch for three key nutrients: Omega-3, Vitamins B12 and D3. Consume Flax/Chia seed every day. Get sunshine regularly and take vitamin B-12 supplements. No other Supplements.
6. Keep your body hydrated at all times. Drink minimum 1/2 liter warm water first thing in the morning. One glass an hour after each meal and one glass before going to bed. Eat your water as much as possible.
7. Practice 16:8 Intermittent Fasting. Finish dinner before it gets dark outside and at least 3 hours before bedtime.
8. Keep a 36-40 hour (Ekadashi) fast every two weeks and one or two 9 day OMAD (Navratri) fast each year.
9. Stay active: Walk 10,000 steps or more daily.
10. Keep your circadian clock in Sync. Sleep a minimum of 7-9 hours daily.
11. Love your family and friends unconditionally and stay connected.
12. Pray regularly and have a positive attitude full of gratitude. That you pray is important; who you pray to is irrelevant.
